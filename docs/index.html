LISTENING TESTS

Expression of pitch
in contemporary popular music

 

The tests are part of a study of pitch in the context of contemporary popular music.

The study is conducted by Aalborg University and Sony CSL Paris. Its aim is to understand how studio music producers manipulate audio content to transmit audible pitch values.

The test duration is 15 minutes overall.

Please keep to the same playback system for all questions!

 

The data is anonymous and collected for academic purposes only.
